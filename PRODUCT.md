# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Sourcing agents and small trading companies in Asia (primarily China) managing 3-20 active client relationships. They currently coordinate via WhatsApp groups and Excel sheets. Their clients are importers in Europe and North America who expect professional reporting but receive WhatsApp screenshots instead. Primary context: working alone or in a small team, under pressure, late at night, closing deals across time zones.

## Product Purpose

SourSync replaces the WhatsApp + Excel workflow for sourcing agents. It centralizes quotations, orders, shipping tracking, and payments in one platform with a client-facing portal. Success looks like: an agent sending a client a clean portal link instead of a screenshot of a spreadsheet. The product is a serious B2B operations tool, not a consumer app.

## Brand Personality

Precise, authoritative, frictionless. The brand should feel like a serious instrument — operator-grade, like the Bloomberg terminal for sourcing. Competence over delight. Confidence over friendliness. The product earns trust through visual precision, not softness.

## Anti-references

- Notion-style: soft, friendly, beige — SourSync is for operators, not note-takers
- Crypto / Web3: neon on black, chaotic energy — sourcing is professional B2B
- Generic SaaS template with dark bg + timid cyan accent + uniform icon-card grid
- Glassmorphism as decoration
- Gradient text
- Identical card grids (icon + heading + text, repeated)
- Large rounded-corner icons above every heading

## Design Principles

1. Show the product, not a promise. The dashboard screenshot is the hero; copy supports it.
2. Operator-grade confidence. No hedging, no "simple and easy" softness. The product is powerful.
3. Earn every pixel. Remove elements that don't convert. Whitespace is not emptiness.
4. Trust through precision. Clean grids, exact alignment, deliberate hierarchy signal reliability.
5. Committed color. Cyan (#06b6d4) is THE brand color — used with conviction in key moments, not scattered everywhere as decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Multilingual (EN, FR, AR, ZH) with full RTL layout support for Arabic via Tailwind and the Tajawal font. Chinese via Noto Sans SC. Reduced motion should be respected where possible.
